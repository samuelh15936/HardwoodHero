Our Superhero Services

Dustless Sanding

Our state-of-the-art dustless sanding technology ensures a clean, mess-free process.

Our dustless sanding process uses advanced equipment to capture 99% of the dust created during the sanding process. This means:

  • A cleaner home during and after the process
  • Reduced allergens and irritants in the air
  • Faster completion times with less cleanup
  • Better adhesion for stains and finishes

Installation

Expert installation of various hardwood flooring types to suit your style and budget.

Our installation services include:

  • Professional assessment of your subfloor
  • Wide selection of hardwood species and styles
  • Precise installation techniques for long-lasting results
  • Thorough cleanup and walk-through after completion

Refinishing

Bring new life to your existing hardwood floors with our professional refinishing services.

Our refinishing process includes:

  • Thorough cleaning and preparation of existing floors
  • Repair of any damaged areas
  • Multiple sanding stages for a smooth finish
  • Application of high-quality stains and finishes

Custom Staining

Choose from a wide range of stain colors to perfectly match your home's aesthetic.

Our custom staining service offers:

  • Extensive color options to match any decor
  • Custom color mixing for unique shades
  • Sample testing to ensure your satisfaction
  • Expert application for consistent, beautiful results
